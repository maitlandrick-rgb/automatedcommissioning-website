How It Works

Three Steps. Zero Guesswork.

We connect securely to your existing building control system, run tests automatically in the background, and deliver a plain-English report straight to your inbox — every week, without you lifting a finger.

1
🔗

Connect

We establish a secure link to your browser-based Building Automation System (BAS) — the computer that controls your building's heating, cooling, and ventilation. No new hardware required. Works locally or remotely.

2
⚙️

Test Automatically

Every week, our system runs hundreds of automated checks on all your equipment — fans, heating zones, cooling units, room pressures, airflow rates, and more. It works silently in the background without disrupting operations.

3
📋

Receive Your Report

You get a clear, prioritized email report showing exactly what passed, what needs attention, and what your team should fix first. No technical jargon — just actionable information your staff can act on immediately.

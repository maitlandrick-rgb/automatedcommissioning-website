The Process

Simple Setup. Automatic Insight. Every Week.

We connect to your building's existing control system, run comprehensive tests automatically, and deliver a clear report to your inbox every week.

1
🔗

Step 1: Secure Connection

We establish a secure, read-only connection to your Building Automation System (BAS). No new hardware required. Works locally or remotely.

🔒 Read-Only Access

We never modify settings or control logic. We only read data.

🌐 Local or Remote

Works within your network or via secure remote connection.

⚙️ No Hardware

Uses your existing BAS infrastructure. Nothing to physically install.

⚡ Quick Discovery

We map your data points and equipment in our first session.

2
⚙️

Step 2: Automated Weekly Testing

Every week, our system runs the same checks a commissioning engineer would perform manually — automatically, consistently, without human error. We test heating sequences, cooling responses, fan operation, airflow rates, alarms, and more.

📅 Weekly Schedule

Tests run automatically overnight so nothing impacts your operations.

📈 Trend Tracking

We track performance over time to catch gradual degradation early.

🎪 Background Operation

Tests run silently. Staff and occupants notice nothing.

🔧 Custom Test Plans

Tests tailored to your specific equipment and operational priorities.

3
📋

Step 3: Your Weekly Report Arrives

Every Monday morning, a clear, prioritized deficiency report lands in your inbox. It shows what passed, what failed, how long the issue has been present, and what your team should do about it — in plain language, not engineering jargon.

📧 Monday Morning Delivery

Consistent reporting so your team can plan for the week.

🔎 Prioritized Issues

Problems ranked by severity so you know what to fix first.

💻 Dashboard Access

All data accessible anytime through your secure online dashboard.

🎯 Actionable Notes

Plain-English notes so any staff member can understand and act.

Equipment We Monitor

We Test All of It

🌫️

Air Handling Units (AHUs)

Supply/return fans, heating/cooling coils, damper operation

🔌

VAV Boxes

Airflow rates, heating sequences, damper positions, zone temps

🏠

RTUs (Rooftop Units)

Cooling stages, heating stages, fan operation, supply air temps

💨

Exhaust & Supply Fans

Command vs. status feedback, runtime, flow verification

🌮

Chillers & Cooling Towers

Chilled water temps, condenser flow, tower fans, alarms

💧

Boilers & Pumps

Hot water temps, pump operation, differential pressure

🌡️

Space & Zone Data

Temperatures, humidity, CO2, differential pressure (critical for medical)

🚨

Alarms & Faults

Active alarms, alarm history, communication faults

📋

Custom Sequences

Any specialized control logic specific to your facility
